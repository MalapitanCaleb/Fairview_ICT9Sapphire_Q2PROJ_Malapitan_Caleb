Tinola


Tinola is a Filipino soup usually served as a main course with white rice. Traditionally, this dish is cooked
with chicken or fish, wedges of papaya and/or chayote, and leaves of the siling labuyo
chili pepper in broth flavored with ginger, onions and fish sauce.

INGREDIENTS
Chicken 3 lbs.
Green papaya, wedged 2 PCS.
Malunggay leaves 1 1/2 cup
Hot pepper leaves 1 cup
Garlic, crushed and chopped 5 Cloves
Onion, chopped 1 PCS.
Ginger, julienned 3 Thumbs
Maggi Magic Chicken Cubes 2 PCS.
Water 1 Quart
Rice wash 1 Quart
Patis 2 Tbsp.
Ground pepper(optional) To taste
Cooking oil 3 Tbsp.

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat oil in a pan. When oil is hot add onion and garlic. Cook for about 2 mins or until fragrant.
  2. Add ginger and fish sauce; cook for 1-2 mins and then add chicken. Stir and cook chicken for 5 mins.
  3. Add chicken broth and let it cook for 5 mins. Add squash and simmer until the chicken is cooked, about 10 mins. Season with some salt and pepper..
  4. Add bok choy and spinach; cook until spinach is just wilted, 1 to 2 minutes. Serve hot.
