Champorado


Champorado is a sweet chocolate rice porridge that uses sweet glutinous rice
(locally known as malagkit) and cocoa powder as main ingredients.

INGREDIENTS
Tablea(Pure Chocolate) 5 PCS.
Sticky Rice 1 3/4 Cups
White Sugar 3/4 Cups
Water 6-8 Cups
Condensed To taste

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Pour water in a cooking pot. Bring to a boil.
  2. add the tablea and let it dissolve in boiling water.
  3. Add he rice and let the water re-boil. Stir constantly to avoid sticking. When the rice absorbs the water it should be ready, about 15-25 mins.
  4. Add the sugar. Stir until the sugar dissolves.
  5. Plate and its ready to serve! Note: Feel free to add condensed milk or sugar for taste.
